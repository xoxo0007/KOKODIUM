What is KOKODIUM intelligence?

KOKODIUM intelligence is an online resource about secret service and hacking manuals that is loosely associated with anonymous. Our goal is to provide manuals that enable you to learn how intelligence organisations work, analyse, agitate, interrogate and so forth. Knowing much more about such things can help you to do political analysis, spot intelligence officers, and best of all, spot possible intelligence operations in the news. There's also books on computer hacking, coding and a fun section (misc) that might be useful.

I am providing material here that is hard to get without being tracked in one or the other way. Take it as an opportunity to dive a bit into the how-abouts of services and hacking.
